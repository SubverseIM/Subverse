Welcome to Subverse!

Subverse is OUR network, an internet protocol standard designed to put the power in your hands, and decentralize the modern Internet.

Who is this for?

Currently, if you are trying to get started with your own Subverse "hub" instance, look for the documentation included with your server distribution package. In it, you should find a section regarding "Bootstrapping", be sure to read that guidance, and when applicable, use the URL of this page to configure your instance's bootstrapper API server.
